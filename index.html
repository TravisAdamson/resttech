Built for the Funeral Profession

REST stands for Remembrance End-of-Life Services and Tracking. It represents our mission: creating technology that helps funeral professionals organize information, manage services, and serve families with confidence.

REST combines case management, contracts, accounting, reporting, and record organization into one platform designed specifically for funeral homes.
